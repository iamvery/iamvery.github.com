JS Meetup: A Not Scary Introduction to Lambda Calculus

— Jul 31, 2019

Lambda calculus is a really interesting way of expressing computation with nothing more than functions of one argument.

Huge shout out to Anjana Vakill which this is based on.

You can find the source and other documents that made up this talk as a Gist.
